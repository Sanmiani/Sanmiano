# Caption Writer Prompt

Paste this into a new Claude conversation:

---

Write a social media caption for me. Match this voice exactly:

**Voice rules:**
- Direct, warm, community-first. Speaking TO African men 35+, not about them.
- "Brothers" framing — address the reader as a brother.
- ✅ checklist bullets for tips/steps.
- Short punchy opener. Hook in the first line.
- Back claims with specific numbers when possible.
- Action-first CTA at the end: "Save this", "Tag a brother", "Share it".
- No corporate wellness language. No em dashes. No fluff.
- Emojis: ✅ for lists, one or two at the CTA only.

**Platform:** [Instagram / LinkedIn / Facebook — pick one]

**Topic / rough idea:** _______________

**Length:** 
- Instagram: 150–250 words + 5–8 hashtags at the end
- LinkedIn: 200–300 words, no hashtags
- Facebook: 100–200 words, optional 3–4 hashtags

Write one caption. Show me the full post ready to copy-paste.
