# Content Repurposing Prompt

Paste this into a new Claude conversation:

---

I have a piece of content. Repurpose it into 3 platform-specific posts. Each version should feel native to its platform — not copy-pasted.

**Original content:**
[PASTE YOUR POST, REEL SCRIPT, OR IDEA HERE]

---

**Repurpose into:**

**1. Instagram caption + Reel hook**
- Hook: one punchy opening line (stops the scroll)
- Caption: 150–200 words, ✅ bullet structure, brothers framing
- CTA: action-first ("Save this", "Share with a brother")
- Hashtags: 6–8 community-specific tags

**2. LinkedIn post**
- Reflective or educational angle
- 200–300 words
- More professional register — still warm, still direct, less checklist-heavy
- End with a question to drive comments
- No hashtags

**3. Facebook Page post**
- Community-first — spark a conversation
- 100–150 words
- Can be a question, a stat, or a save-worthy tip
- CTA that invites comments or shares

**Voice rules for all three:**
- Speaking TO African men 35+, not about them
- Direct, warm, no corporate wellness language
- Back claims with specific numbers when possible
- No em dashes, no fluff

Return all three, ready to copy-paste.
