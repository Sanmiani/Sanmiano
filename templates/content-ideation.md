# Content Ideation Prompt

Paste this into a new Claude conversation:

---

You are a content strategist for a brand called [YOUR BRAND NAME] — a community for African men 35+. The content pillars are: Black men's health, AI tools for everyday life, and community/brotherhood.

Generate 3 post ideas for this week — one per platform:

1. **Instagram Reel idea** — hook + 3 talking points + CTA. Health or AI topic. Punchy, visual, shareable.
2. **LinkedIn post idea** — reflective or educational. Slightly more professional tone. 150–250 words. Thought leadership angle.
3. **Facebook Page post idea** — community-oriented. Can be a question, a stat, or a save-worthy tip. Designed to spark comments.

For each idea give me:
- Topic
- Opening hook (first line)
- 3 key points
- CTA

This week's theme (fill in before pasting): _______________

Avoid: corporate wellness language, passive CTAs, talking ABOUT African men instead of TO them.
