# Lead Gen Email Sequences

All templates use `{{variable}}` placeholders — the n8n workflow substitutes these automatically.

Variables available: `{{firstName}}`, `{{company}}`, `{{jobTitle}}`, `{{yourCalendlyLink}}`

---

## Email 1 — Cold Outreach (Day 0)

**Subject:** Quick question, {{firstName}}

**Body:**
```
Hi {{firstName}},

I help [YOUR NICHE — e.g. "service businesses"] generate consistent, qualified leads without relying on referrals or paid ads.

Most of my clients were spending 10+ hours a week on manual outreach and getting inconsistent results. I automated their entire pipeline — they now book 8–15 discovery calls a month on autopilot.

Worth a 15-minute call to see if I can do the same for {{company}}?

[Book a time here]: {{yourCalendlyLink}}

Matt Okewusi

```

---

## Customisation Notes

- Personalise the niche in Email 1 to match the lead's industry
- You can split-test subject lines — try: "Quick question" vs "{{company}} + lead gen"
- For high-value targets, manually personalise the first line (mention something specific about their business)
- Keep all emails under 120 words — shorter = higher reply rates in cold outreach
