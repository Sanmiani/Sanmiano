# Sunday Batch — Weekly Content Workflow

## Overview

Run this every Sunday. Input one weekly theme, get three ready-to-post pieces:
- **Monday** — Instagram Reel script + caption (health or AI focus)
- **Wednesday** — LinkedIn post (professional, reflective register)
- **Friday** — Facebook post (community-first, discussion prompt)

Each piece is written in Matt's voice, platform-adapted, with hook, body, CTA, and hashtags.

---

## When to use

- Every Sunday before the week starts
- Any time Matt needs to batch content fast (travel week, busy period)
- When content ideas are dry — the skill handles ideation too

---

## Step 1 — Intake (ask once, generate everything)

Ask Matt ONE compound question:

> "What's the weekly theme — or should I suggest one based on your priorities? And is there a stat, story, or specific angle you want to anchor it to?"

If he has no idea: suggest 3 theme options drawn from the content pillars (Health, AI & Tools, Brotherhood). Let him pick.

**Content pillars:**
- **Health** — Blood pressure, sleep, testosterone, nutrition, mental health, prostate health, diabetes, fitness after 35
- **AI & Tools** — Specific tools Matt uses, productivity wins, time savings, AI for health tracking
- **Brotherhood** — Community stories, accountability, African men in diaspora, silence around men's health

Do NOT ask follow-up questions. Generate immediately after theme is confirmed.

---

## Step 2 — Generate all three posts

Generate Posts 1, 2, and 3 back to back in a single response. Use the templates below. Write in Matt's voice — see register rules at the bottom.

---

### POST 1 — Instagram (Monday)

**Format:**

```
📅 MONDAY · INSTAGRAM REEL

--- REEL SCRIPT (spoken, 30–45 sec) ---
[Hook line — spoken directly to camera. Bold claim or shocking stat.]
[3 action points — short, punchy, one sentence each]
[Closing CTA — one sentence]

--- CAPTION ---
[Opening hook — same energy as spoken hook, 1–2 sentences]

✅ [Point 1 — expanded with specific data]
✅ [Point 2 — expanded with specific data]
✅ [Point 3 — expanded with specific data]

[CTA — "Save this", "Tag a brother", "Drop a comment if..."]

#Hashtag1 #Hashtag2 #Hashtag3 #Hashtag4 #Hashtag5
```

**Rules for Instagram:**
- Hook must create a pattern interrupt. Lead with a stat or a bold "if you're a man 35+..." statement.
- ✅ checklist format always.
- 5–7 hashtags max. Include at least 2 community tags (#AfroMen #BlackMensHealth #StrongAfter35).
- CTA is action-first. Never passive.
- Reel script reads exactly as Matt would speak it — short sentences, no run-ons.

---

### POST 2 — LinkedIn (Wednesday)

**Format:**

```
📅 WEDNESDAY · LINKEDIN

[Hook — 1 sentence. No ✅ bullets. Opens with a personal observation or data point.]

[2–3 short paragraphs — reflective, professional. First person. More story, less checklist.]

[1 paragraph on the AI or modern angle — how tools/data help.]

[Closing thought — invite discussion. End with a question.]

[3–4 hashtags — professional tags: #MensHealth #AITools #AfricanProfessionals #Leadership]
```

**Rules for LinkedIn:**
- Do NOT use the Instagram checklist style here.
- Longer, more reflective sentences. Sounds like a professional sharing hard-won insight.
- Still direct — no corporate wellness language.
- Always end with a question to drive comments.
- Keep it under 1,300 characters for best reach.

---

### POST 3 — Facebook (Friday)

**Format:**

```
📅 FRIDAY · FACEBOOK PAGE

[Opening — conversational. "Brothers," or "Quick question for the community:"]

[2–3 short paragraphs — mix of info and community framing. Feels like a group discussion starter.]

[Discussion CTA — "Drop a comment", "Tell me below", "Share this if your brother needs to hear it"]

[2–3 hashtags only]
```

**Rules for Facebook:**
- Most conversational of the three. Like Matt is talking to brothers directly.
- Shorter than LinkedIn. Less formal than Instagram captions.
- End with a question or prompt that pulls comments. Facebook rewards discussion.
- Works as a companion piece to the Monday Instagram post — same theme, different angle.

---

## Step 3 — Output format

After all three posts, print this block:

```
---
WEEK OF: [date range, e.g. May 11–16, 2026]
THEME: [theme]
PILLAR: [Health / AI & Tools / Brotherhood]

CHECKLIST:
☐ Monday — Post Instagram Reel + caption
☐ Wednesday — Post LinkedIn
☐ Friday — Post Facebook
☐ Engage comments within 1 hour of posting (Instagram algorithm rewards early engagement)
---
```

Then ask: "Want me to also write the Reel on-screen text overlays, or draft a Canva graphic prompt for any of these?"

---

## Voice register rules (non-negotiable)

From `references/voice.md`:

- **"Brothers" framing** — speaks from within the community, not about it
- **✅ bullets with specific numbers** — "4,700mg", "30% higher risk", "150 minutes" — no vague claims
- **Short punchy openers** — no warm-up, no "In today's post I want to talk about"
- **Action-first CTAs** — "Save this", "Tag a brother", "Share it" — never "feel free to like"
- **No em dashes in casual posts** — use short sentences instead
- **No corporate wellness language** — never "holistic", "wellness journey", "self-care routine"
- **LinkedIn is NOT Instagram** — drop the ✅ format, use reflective paragraphs
- **Do not fake urgency** — his urgency is real and data-backed, let the numbers speak

---

## What NOT to generate

- Generic "Top 10 tips" posts with no African-specific data
- Content that sounds like it could be for any man, any age, any culture
- Passive CTAs ("Like and follow for more content")
- LinkedIn posts that look like Instagram captions
- Any post without a specific stat or data point backing the main claim
