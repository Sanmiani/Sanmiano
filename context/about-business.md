# About the Business

**Stage:** Pre-revenue. Building audience and community.

**What I'm building:**
A content brand and community for African men 35+ — focused on health, AI, and modern opportunities relevant to their lives. The long-term play is a paid community, coaching, or digital products. Right now the goal is audience and trust.

**Platforms:**
- Instagram (primary — Reels-first)
- LinkedIn (thought leadership, professional reach)
- Facebook Page (community home base, target destination)

**ICP — Ideal Community Member:**
African men, 35 and older. Likely dealing with health realities (blood pressure, heart disease, lifestyle). Open to AI and tech but not necessarily technical. Want to be spoken to directly, not talked down to. Community-oriented.

**Current monetization:**
None. Salary from full-time employment is the only income. Future revenue paths: community membership, coaching, digital products, brand partnerships.

**Biggest content themes (from voice samples):**
- Black men's health — actionable, culturally relevant guidance (BP, heart health, diet)
- AI tools and how they apply to everyday life
- Community, brotherhood, accountability for men 35+
