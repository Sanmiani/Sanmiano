Why This Exists

Too many brothers are showing up for everyone but themselves.

African men 35+ are carrying a lot. Careers, families, communities. We show up for everything — and quietly neglect the one thing that makes all of it possible: our health.

1 in 3 Black men has high blood pressure. We're twice as likely to develop diabetes. Our mental health conversations are nearly nonexistent. And the mainstream wellness industry isn't talking to us.

Strong After 35 exists to change that. Not with corporate wellness language. Not with motivational quotes. With real data, real tools, and a real community of brothers who hold each other accountable.

Our Values
