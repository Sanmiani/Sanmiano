## Your prompt (copy everything below this line)

---

You are helping Matt Sanmiano prepare a weekly posting pack for his brand Strongafter35afrimen — a content brand for African men 35+, focused on health, AI, and modern opportunities.

**Audience:** African men, 35 and older. Direct. Community-first. No corporate wellness language.

**Voice rules:**
- "Brothers" framing. "35+" as an identity marker.
- Short punchy sentences. No em dashes in casual posts.
- Back claims with numbers, not vibes.
- Emojis used purposefully: ✅ for lists, 💪💚📲 at CTAs.

---

### OUTPUT A — Instagram Caption (for the Reel)

This goes in the caption field below the Reel video. It is NOT the spoken script — it's what people read when they pause to look at the post.

Format:
- 1 punchy opener line that echoes the Reel hook (make them want to watch)
- 2-3 lines summarising the value inside the Reel
- 1 CTA line — save, share, or comment
- 5-10 hashtags — mix reach tags (#AfricanMen #BlackMensHealth #MenOver35) with topic tags
- Keep total under 150 words

---

### OUTPUT B — LinkedIn Post

Adapt the Facebook post for LinkedIn. Same topic, more professional register — but still direct and personal. Matt is positioning himself as an AI Specialist and health advocate for African men 35+.

Format:
- Bold opener line (no all-caps title needed)
- 2-3 short paragraphs: hook → insight → why it matters to the reader
- 1 closing line that invites engagement ("What's your experience with this?" or similar)
- 3-5 hashtags max — professional tags (#MensHealth #AfricanProfessionals #AIForHealth #HealthOver35)
- Keep total under 200 words. No bullet-point lists — LinkedIn rewards paragraphs.

---

### OUTPUT C — Weekly Posting Schedule

Build a 3-post schedule for the coming week using the content above. Assume Matt is posting Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday.

Best times for African men 35+ (professionals, full-time employed):
- Tuesday: 7:00 AM or 7:00 PM local time
- Thursday: 7:00 AM or 7:00 PM local time
- Saturday: 9:00 AM or 12:00 PM local time

Format as a table:

| Day | Platform | Format | Post | Time |
|-----|----------|--------|------|------|
| Tuesday | Facebook | Text post | [first line of Facebook post] | 7:00 PM |
| Thursday | Instagram | Reel | [Reel topic summary] | 7:00 PM |
| Saturday | LinkedIn | Post | [first line of LinkedIn post] | 9:00 AM |

Add a note below the table: "Substack article can go out same week as the Thursday Reel — it feeds the depth-seekers who want more after seeing the short-form."
